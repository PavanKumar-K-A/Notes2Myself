# Problem: Largest Prime Factor

### Description
- The prime factors of 13195 are 5, 7, 13 and 29.
  What is the largest prime factor of the number 600851475143?
- [Project Euler Problem 3](https://projecteuler.net/problem=3)
- Solution: 6857
